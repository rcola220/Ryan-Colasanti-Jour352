About Me

Ryan Colasanti is a Sports Journalism student writer for WMUC Sports. He covers University of Maryland sports such as volleyball, baseball, and wrestling. He does radio commentary for other University of Maryland sports throughout the year for WMUC Sports as well.

He is currently in the process of earning his bachelor degrees in both journalism and business management from the University of Maryland.

When Ryan is not working he is most likely watching the Yankees or some other sporting event or is gaming on his Play Station 5.
